Rocío Valdebenito

Research: Economic of Education, Microeconomics and Applied microeconomics.

Reseña:

Rocío Valdebenito is Ingeniero Comercial y Master en Management Science at the Universidad Adolfo Ibáñez. Since August 2014 she is a associate research of CEPR, where he works on topics of professional technical education and the transition of these students to higher education.

It also focuses its analysis on regional economy, mainly regional inequalities around quality of life and education. He has been a research assistant in the “Regional Competitiveness Index” by the Center for Public Systems of the University of Chile, and he did a research stay at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign. His subjects of interest are the economics of education, applied microeconomics, regional economics and spatial econometrics.
