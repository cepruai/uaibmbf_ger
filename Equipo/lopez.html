Reseña:

Esteban López is actually a postdoctorate of Centro de Economía y Política Regional (CEPR) and a Assistant Professor of the Economy Deparment at the Universidad Adolfo Ibáñez. He obtained his PhD in Uband an Regional Planning in 2016 and a Master of Science in Applied and Agricultural Economics, both from the University of Illinois at Urbana- Champaign in the United States. Esteban has worked as a consultant for the Lincoln Institute of Land Policy and the REAL-UIUC. His interests of research are focused in general terms on the study of the behavior of economic variables in the territory (spatial / urban economy). Specifically, its research areas cover housing markets, the effects of national policies across regions, migration patterns, among others.
