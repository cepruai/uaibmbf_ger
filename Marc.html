Marc Ingo Wolter

Research: Structural Change, Labour market, Demographic change, Input-output modelling, Regional economics
Education: Dr. Economics at the University of Osnabrück, 2001, Economics at the University of Osnabrück, 1996
Contact: wolter at gws-os.com
Web
About: Marc Ingo Wolter is head of the GWS division ‘Economic and Social Affairs’. He is responsible for predominantly short and long-term projects for public as well as private clients. Marc Ingo Wolter has extensive experience in the development and appliance of macro-economic models as well as in establishing empirical evaluations. His research focus lies on socio-economic development on the background of economic and structural demographic change. He is responsible for the GWS’s industry forecasts. Besides, he is taking part in the socio-economic reporting (www.soeb.de) and is partner of the QuBe project (www.qube-projekt.de).
